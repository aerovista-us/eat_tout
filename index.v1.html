Prep Basics (applies to all 3 methods)

What to buy

  • Trout (whole or fillets). If whole: ask for gutted + scaled if possible.
  • Salt + pepper (kosher salt is a cheat code).
  • Lemon + butter (or olive oil).
  • Garlic (fresh or powder), fresh dill or parsley.
  • Optional flavor squad: paprika, Cajun seasoning, capers, honey, soy sauce.

How to prepare

  • Pin bones: run your fingers along the fillet; pull with tweezers/pliers.
  • Skin on is your friend (protects the fish, crisps up, reduces sticking).
  • Season simply: salt + pepper + lemon + butter is already elite.
  • Rest 10 min after seasoning if you can ‚Äî the salt does its magic.
